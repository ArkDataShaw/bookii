Things we will never do

These are commitments, not marketing. They exist because every one of them is a documented reason people leave other scheduling tools.

  • Never cap event types. One event type on a free plan is a demo, not a plan.
  • Never show ads to your invitees. Your booking page is your reputation.
  • Never charge per connected calendar within a tier. Multiple calendars is an identity, not a usage metric.
  • Never gate reminders. Reminders are how meetings actually happen.
  • Never surprise-renew. Renewal reminder emails, one-click cancel, pro-rated refunds.
  • Never dark your booking pages over billing. If payment fails, your pages stay live while we sort it out — your invitees are never the hostage.
