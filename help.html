Getting started

Set up your page

Sign up at from.bookii.to, claim your link (from.bookii.to/you), and you'll start with Mon–Fri 9–5 availability and two starter event types. Edit anything from the dashboard — every event type has a live preview.

Share a booking link

Every event type has a Copy link button. Your profile link (from.bookii.to/you) lists all your visible event types; each event also has its own direct link. Hidden events stay bookable by direct link but aren't listed.

Connect your calendar

Calendars → Connect. Busy times from every connected calendar are removed from your availability, and new bookings are written to the calendar you mark as destination. Google support is rolling out now; Microsoft and Apple are next.

Availability

Why isn't a time showing on my page?

Use the Troubleshooter on the Availability page: pick the event type and date, and it shows every candidate slot with the exact reason it's offered or blocked — working hours, minimum notice, booking window, buffers, existing bookings, or connected- calendar busy time.

Different hours for different events

Create multiple schedules on the Availability page (e.g. "Working hours," "Evenings") and attach the right one to each event type in its Scheduling rules.

Block a day off

Availability → Date overrides → pick the date → "Unavailable all day." Overrides replace your weekly hours for that date only.

Bookings

Reschedule or cancel a meeting (invitees)

Use the links on your confirmation screen or email. Rescheduling keeps the same booking — just a new time. If the host has disabled these options you'll see a note to contact them directly.

Buffers, notice, and caps

Each event type's Scheduling rules set buffer time before/after meetings, minimum notice, how far ahead people can book, slot spacing, and a max meetings-per-day cap.

Mark attendance

Meetings → status dropdown: attended, no-show, or cancelled. This feeds your show-rate stat.

Embedding

Put booking on your own site

Event type → Embed on your site → copy the inline iframe (page appears in your site) or popup button code. Add ?name=&email= to any booking link to prefill the form.

Account

Change my link/username

Settings → Your link. Careful: changing it breaks links you've already shared.

Delete my account

Settings → Danger zone. Removes your page, event types, schedules, connected calendars, and booking history, and revokes calendar access. Permanent.
